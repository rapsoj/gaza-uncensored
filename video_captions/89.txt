In Jabalia's Ezbet Abd Rabbo, a man stood in shock, pleading for help to transport the body of his wife, lying lifeless on the ground after she was martyred in an Israeli strike.
All his children had also been martyred. But with ambulances in short supply, priority was given to the wounded. People kept fleeing... everyone was running from death, and he was left to carry his grief alone.
