"Just 30 minutes... there's no transportation"
Ahmad Hassouna receives a call from an Israeli officer, ordering him to evacuate his home before the neighboring house is bombed.
Thirty minutes — that's all he has to save himself, his children, and his family.
In the heart of the chaos, he scrambles to protect them from a possible massacre.
A single moment filled with fear, panic, tension... and the nearness of death.
