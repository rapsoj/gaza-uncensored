A child with special needs clears rubble from the remains of his home
With a weary body and unbreakable determination, this child tries to remove the traces of bombing from the place that was once his refuge.
His disability didn't stop him from standing amid the destruction-his humanity stood as a witness to a pain beyond words.
In Gaza, even the most vulnerable are forced to be strong.
